Nelson Mandela
Words Of A True Leader

President of South Africa from 1994 to 1999

I have dedicated my life to this struggle of the African people. I have fought against

white domination, and I have fought against black domination.

I have cherished the ideal of a democratic and free society in which all persons

will live together in harmony and with equal opportunities. It is an ideal for which

I hope to live for and to see realized. But, My Lord, if it needs to be, it is an ideal for which

I am prepared to die.

-Nelson Mandela
